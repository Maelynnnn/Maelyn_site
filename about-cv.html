About

I am currently pursuing a degree in Interactive Media Arts at NYU Shanghai, with a minor in Game Design from the NYU Game Center. My background lies at the intersection of design and technology, focusing particularly on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), game development, and dynamic web environments.

My interdisciplinary expertise spans web development, AI product design, industrial design, and immersive media (XR/AR/MR). I have full-cycle project experience that encompasses AI interaction design, user experience research, full-stack development, and data visualization. I have received multiple international design awards and hold a design patent issued by the China National Intellectual Property Administration (CNIPA).

I also have extensive teaching experience. At NYU Shanghai, I served as a Learning Assistant for the Creative Coding Lab, providing real-time technical support to students. Additionally, I hosted game design workshops, where I introduced core principles and guided students in prototyping and modifying their own board games.
